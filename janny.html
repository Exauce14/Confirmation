Dear Janny,

Today is a day of grace and joy.

In our New Apostolic Church, confirmation is not simply a milestone, but a blessing that God grants you to strengthen you and walk beside you in your life of faith.

From this day forward, you are called to take personal responsibility for your faith: to choose God, to seek Him, and to walk with Him.

Do not be afraid to have your own experiences with God. Seek Him sincerely, and you will discover that He is alive, that He answers, and that He is at work in your life.

Always remember: Jesus Christ is with you. He will never abandon you.

“And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age.”

— Matthew 28:20

May this blessing accompany you every day and guide you throughout your life.

With love and blessings,

Deacon Exaucé NGOLO
